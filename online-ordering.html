About This Service

Take Back Control of Your Orders

Deliveroo, Uber Eats, and Just Eat can be useful for discoverability — but handing over 25–35% of every order is unsustainable. J.P. Nadar builds you a branded online ordering system that you own completely.

Your customers order directly from your website or app, with no commission going to a third party. Every pound stays with you. Orders flow straight into your POS and kitchen — no manual entry, no delays, no errors.

We handle everything: design, setup, menu configuration, payment integration, and delivery zone mapping. Your platform is live within 5 working days, fully branded to match your restaurant's identity.
