Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

1. Introduction

Welcome to J.P. Nadar ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the personal information you provide to us when using our website and services, in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. The Data We Collect

When you fill out a contact form or request a quote on our website, we collect the following personal information so that we can respond to your enquiry:

  • Identity Data: First name and last name.
  • Contact Data: Email address and telephone number.
  • Business Data: Restaurant name, postcode, monthly turnover, and transaction volume (if requesting a card machine quote).
  • Message Data: Any additional information you choose to provide in the message box.

3. How We Use Your Data

We will only use your personal data for the purposes for which we collected it. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Respond to your direct enquiries and provide customer support.
  • Provide you with accurate quotes for our POS systems, card machines, and other restaurant solutions.
  • Follow up regarding the services you have expressed interest in.

We rely on your explicit consent (provided via the checkbox on our contact forms) as the lawful basis for processing this data.

4. How We Store and Share Your Data

We take the security of your data seriously. When you submit a form on our website, your information is securely transmitted to and stored within Google Workspace (Google Sheets), which complies with strict international security standards.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is only accessed by our internal team to assist with your enquiry.

5. Third-Party Services and Cookies

Our website utilizes a limited number of third-party services to function securely:

  • Google reCAPTCHA: We use Google reCAPTCHA (Invisible) to protect our contact forms from spam and abuse. This service analyzes website traffic and user behavior. Your use of reCAPTCHA is subject to the Google Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.

6. Your Data Protection Rights

Under the GDPR, you have several rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access: You can request copies of your personal data.
  • The right to rectification: You can request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate.
  • The right to erasure: You can request that we erase your personal data from our systems.
  • The right to withdraw consent: You can withdraw your consent for us to process your data at any time.

If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.
