About Card Machines

Payments That Never Let You Down

A failed payment at checkout is one of the most frustrating experiences for both customers and staff. J.P. Nadar's card machines are built for high-volume restaurant environments — fast, reliable, and always connected.

Our terminals integrate seamlessly with your J.P. Nadar POS system. When a customer pays, the amount is transferred automatically from the POS to the card terminal — no manual entry, no errors, no discrepancies at end-of-day reconciliation.

All terminals are PCI DSS compliant and support the latest security standards. We handle the payment gateway setup, terminal configuration, and banking integration — you just plug in and take payments from day one.
